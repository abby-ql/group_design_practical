# Ethics & safety notes (student demonstrator)

This project outputs **risk indicators**, not judgments of a person.

Minimum guardrails for the demo:
- Operate only on **user-provided / consented** data.
- Provide clear, structured **explanations** for every flag.
- Avoid sensitive inference (e.g., don't infer protected characteristics).
- When a term is **ambiguous**, say so and provide disambiguation hints.
- For quoted content or sarcasm markers, prefer **caution** (don't over-penalise).

The rule-based toxicity list in this pack is intentionally mild and incomplete to avoid embedding harmful lexicons in student repositories.
